Air Conditioning FAQ

How often should I service my air conditioner?

It's recommended to service your air conditioner at least once a year to ensure optimal performance and longevity. For heavy usage, consider bi-annual maintenance.

How do I know what size AC unit I need?

The size of your AC unit depends on the area you need to cool. A professional technician can calculate the required cooling capacity based on your room's square footage and insulation.

What are the benefits of an energy-efficient AC?

Energy-efficient air conditioners save on electricity bills, reduce environmental impact, and often come with advanced features like smart temperature controls.

What is the average lifespan of an air conditioning unit?

Most air conditioners last between 10-15 years with proper maintenance. Upgrading to a newer model can improve efficiency and save costs over time.

What should I do if my AC is leaking water?

Water leakage usually occurs due to clogged condensate drains or dirty filters. Turn off the unit and contact a professional technician for assistance.
